Classic Overnight Oats

Description

A fantastic grab-and-go breakfast that is easy and incredibly versatile.

  1. Prep time: 8 minutes
  2. Cook time: 0 minutes
  3. Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 85 mL (1⁄3 cup) lower fat plain yogurt
  • 85 mL (1⁄3 cup) large flake oats
  • 85 mL (1⁄3 cup) lower fat milk, unsweetened fortified soy beverage or water
  • 125 mL (1⁄2 cup) frozen strawberries
  • 2 mL (1⁄2 tsp) cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together all ingredients (except strawberries). Spoon into a jar or container with a tight-fitting lid.
  2. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, but preferably overnight before eating. Top with strawberries when ready to serve.
